Our Story

Made from
scratch,
with care.

Hi, I'm Léa — the baker, delivery driver, and obsessive recipe tester behind Le Cookie. What started as weekend experiments in my tiny kitchen has grown into something I'm incredibly proud of.

Every batch is made to order using locally sourced butter, single-origin chocolate, and a whole lot of patience. No shortcuts, no preservatives — just honest, deeply satisfying cookies.

I believe dessert is an act of love. Whether you're treating yourself or gifting someone special, I put that same care into every single cookie.
